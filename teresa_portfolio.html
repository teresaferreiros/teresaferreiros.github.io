About

I started as a journalist — which means I learned early that complexity is not an excuse for confusion, and that every story worth telling has a human at its centre.

After a decade advising companies at Edelman, I moved in-house to do the same work with more skin in the game. Revolut during European expansion. BitMEX during one of its hardest years. Block, building trust for bitcoin products that had never existed before.

I specialise in the intersection of financial technology and trust — the moment when a complex, unfamiliar, or scrutinised product needs to become something a journalist will care about, a regulator will take seriously, and a customer will truly believe in.

Based in Barcelona. Working globally.

01
Block — Bitkey & Proto

Two launches. One complete narrative pivot. New territory for bitcoin hardware.

Product Launch Bitcoin Global Markets Narrative Pivot Mining Hardware
The challenge

Bitkey launched in March 2024 aimed at mainstream audiences. A year in, the data told a different story: mainstream wasn't that ready for self-custody, but bitcoin-native users — people who already used Ledger, Trezor, Casa, or Unchained — were, and deserved a product like Bitkey. The entire communications strategy, product roadmap, and channel mix had to shift within a few weeks. Simultaneously, leading the global launch of Proto Rig in August 2025 — Block's first modular bitcoin mining hardware, challenging Bitmain's decade-long dominance, and changing the narrative around bitcoin mining through a proprietary content and case study programme spanning media, policy makers, and social channels.

The outcome

The Bitkey pivot delivered: podcast coverage across What Bitcoin Did, Bitcoin Magazine, The Mining Pod, and Bitcoin News drove deep ecosystem engagement in Q4 2025. The brand and team were also present at every major bitcoin-focused event across the US, Europe and EMEA, engaging directly with customers and stakeholders to educate about a new way of owning bitcoin.

Content & editorial

Collaborated on a proprietary content programme for Proto — the Possible Futures case study series on proto.xyz/blog — selecting angles, shaping narratives, and driving distribution to media, policy makers, and regulators. Contributed to the bitkey.build editorial blog, translating product philosophy into content that earned coverage and built ecosystem trust.

The outcome

The Proto launch — held at Core Scientific's facility in Dalton, GA, with Jack Dorsey in attendance — generated widespread international coverage across Bitcoin Magazine, PYMNTS, Fortune, Nasdaq, and the global mining press.

02
Revolut — Spain & Portugal

Building a brand from zero — with data as the story and culture as the hook.

Market Entry Data Campaigns Fintech Spain & Portugal Cultural Campaigns
The challenge

Establish Revolut's voice in Spain — a market where the brand was completely unknown — during one of Europe's most rapid fintech expansions. From 2 to 200 employees in Spain during my time. Build media presence from scratch while supporting product launches, regulatory milestones, and executive positioning simultaneously. Contribute to the global crypto narrative for the brand, and to the Learn & Earn educational platform.

The outcome

Consistent coverage across Spain's leading business and financial media, driven by proprietary spending data campaigns that generated original story angles where none existed. The Pride cards campaign — an advocacy and product moment tying a customised Pride debit card to Revolut's values — landed regionally and became one of the highest-visibility brand moments of the year. Later led interim communications for Portugal and expansion into Mexico.
